What is Armocromy?

Each of us has a color palette that, if used at best, helps us to enhance and valorise our image. Nevertheless not many know they have it. The Armocromia is the study of our image, which helps us to establish which colors best enhance our skin tone, the color of our eyes and our hair.In fact, the correct use of our color palette allows us to have a brighter look.

The color palettes are divided into four main categories and take inspiration from nature and the cyclical passage of time. They are in fact called as the four seasons.

The four Seasons

Winter

Undertone: cool Value: deep Intensity: bright

The Winter palette seems to be the most popular. Characterized by a strong light-dark contrast, it is based on the use of cold, intense and bright colors. Dramatic and vivid colors such as fuchsia and optical white can be its strenght.

Spring

Undertone: warm Value: light Intensity: bright

It is famous for being the most varied color palette in the world. Enemy of cold colors such as black and gray, it prefers warm and bright colors. The Spring subjects who wear them will have a more radiant complexion.

Summer

Undertone: cool Value: light Intensity: soft

The summer palette contains the softest and most delicate colors. Light as a summer breeze, they prefer cold colors like the winter palette, but absolutely soft and not bright. Pastel colors are absolutely recommended.

Fall

Undertone: warm Value: deep Intensity: soft

Warm as autumn leaves, this palette is characterized by soft and deep colors. Inspired by the enveloping shades of wood and spice, it embraces a diverse variety of hair colors, from brunette to redheads through to warm blondes.
